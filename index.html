About Us

We Believe in the Power of
Every African Child

The African Children Initiative was founded in 2009 with a simple but powerful belief: every child born on African soil deserves access to quality education, proper healthcare, nutrition, and a safe environment to grow and thrive.

Over 15 years, we've built schools, trained teachers, funded medical camps, provided clean water, and distributed millions of meals across 32 African nations — all powered by compassionate donors like you.

Stories of Hope

Lives You Have
Already Changed

"Before ACI found me, I had dropped out of school to help my mother sell water by the roadside. Today, I am in my final year of medicine at the University of Lagos. I am proof that one donation can change a destiny."

Amina O. Medical Student, Lagos, Nigeria

"The mobile clinic saved my son's life. He had severe malaria and we couldn't afford the hospital. The ACI team came to our village, treated him for free, and today he is healthy and in school. God bless every donor."

Kofi Mensah Father of 3, Kumasi, Ghana

"I used to walk 8km every day to fetch water. Now we have a borehole right in our village built by ACI. I now have time to study after school. I want to be an engineer and build more water systems for my people."

Fatuma K. Student, Age 14, Nairobi, Kenya
